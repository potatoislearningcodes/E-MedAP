About Us

We are students from 12 STEM Pasteur

Meet our team of dreamers and doers! We're just a group of friends who share a common vision: to make healthcare accessible to all. United by our friendship and fueled by our passion for technology, we embarked on an extraordinary journey to create an e-medicine website paired with an AI chatbot. With diverse skills ranging from coding to design, we combined our strengths to develop a platform that puts healthcare in the palm of your hand. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower individuals to take control of their health journey with ease and confidence. Join us as we blend friendship with innovation to build a healthier, happier world for everyone.
